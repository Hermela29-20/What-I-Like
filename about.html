About Me

Ever since I was a little girl, I’ve been drawn to capturing the world through my lens. What started as a simple curiosity with my phone’s camera has turned into a passion I carry with me every day. Now a second-year campus student, you’ll rarely find me without my camera (or phone) in hand — always ready to freeze a memory, a smile, or a fleeting moment of beauty. Photography isn’t just a hobby for me; it’s how I connect with the world and express what words often can’t.
